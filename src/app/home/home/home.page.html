神隠し

Spirited Away is a manifestation of fears and anxieties as seen through the lens of its young lead female character, Chihiro. How Chihiro chooses to react to the ever-changing circumstances around her, even the most horrifying ones, defines this film's central moral.

Spirited Away Inspiration

Hayao Miyazaki got his inspiration for Spirited Away (Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi)from a group of young women he would spend his summers with. He wanted to make a movie that they could enjoy, that wouldn't be a simple romance as seen in many shoujo manga.
